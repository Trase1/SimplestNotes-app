<i>Simplest Notes</i> is a clean, minimalistic Android app for quickly taking and organizing notes — without ads, clutter, or complexity.

<b>Features:</b>

- 📝 Create, edit, and delete notes
- 🔴 Prioritize notes (Low, Medium, High)
- 💾 Offline-first — no network required to use
- 📤 Lightweight — fast and smooth
- ❤️ Donate via QR code (optional)
